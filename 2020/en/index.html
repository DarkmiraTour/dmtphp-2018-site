JOIN US AT THE CONFERENCE

3 days of learning and sharing with PHP communities


Discoveries

Come and discover the latest features in the PHP ecosystem, best practices, tools and methodologies during these 2 days with more than 30 talks where international and national speakers will share their knowledge in English and Portuguese.

Interactions

Interact with 400 attendees during the various coffee breaks, demonstrations and networking in the 2 communities and sponsors spaces. Share your knowledge in the unconference area where everyone can submit a talk.

Keynotes

This time it's 2 international lectures, that's right, 2.

Nicolas Grekas
Paris, France

Nicolas works like General Manager on Symfony, with two sides to his contributions. On the open-source side, he's been submitting new features and bug fixes almost daily for 5 years.
He puts a lot of efforts on making Symfony fast, easy to use and well designed, all at the same time.

On the business side, he leads the ambition to create a sustainable company supporting and supported directly by the Symfony ecosystem. He previously worked as CTO for Blackfire.io, the PHP profiler that we were all missing and you should definitively try.
