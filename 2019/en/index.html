JOIN US AT THE CONFERENCE

2 days of learning and sharing with PHP communities


Discoveries

Come and discover the latest features in the PHP ecosystem, best practices, tools and methodologies during these 2 days with more than 30 talks where international and national speakers will share their knowledge in English and Portuguese.

Interactions

Interact with 400 attendees during the various coffee breaks, demonstrations and networking in the 2 communities and sponsors spaces. Share your knowledge in the unconference area where everyone can submit a talk.

New place!!

After 3 editions in Brasilia with the PHPDF, a new community will host the next editions. The PHP Com Rapadura community will now host the conference in Fortaleza, capital of the sun and beautiful beaches.
